How to Play

Goal

Destroy all enemy worms before they destroy yours! Each team takes turns firing weapons.

Controls

Move: Tap the ← → arrows to walk your worm left/right.

Jump: Tap JUMP to hop over obstacles. Combine with a direction to jump further.

Aim & Fire: Drag from your worm — the direction and length of the drag sets your aim and power. Release to fire!

Airstrike: Tap anywhere on the map to call in an airstrike at that location.

Weapons

🚀 Bazooka — Reliable rocket. Good range and damage.

💣 Grenade — Bounces before exploding. Tricky but powerful.

🔫 Shotgun — Short range, high damage. Get close!

☤️ Holy Grenade — Massive blast radius. Handle with prayer.

🍌 Banana Bomb — Splits into cluster bombs on impact.

✈️ Airstrike — Bombs rain from above. Tap the target location.

Tips

🌊 Watch the wind — it affects projectile flight!

💨 Explosions destroy terrain — use this to drop enemies into the water.

⏰ You have 30 seconds per turn. If time runs out, your turn ends.

💀 Falling from heights causes damage. Water is instant death!
